ALL ABOUT OPEL

OPEL LED'S

OPEL LED offers a wide range of international quality LED lighting solutions, for varied applications providing effective and energy-efficient lighting in any environment from residential and retail, to commercial and industrial applications. With the LED Lighting market set to reach 85% in India, the OPEL Group realised that this was the cornerstone that the company was to build upon. Now, OPEL LED is a market leader and provides numerous lighting solutions. OPEL LED currently holds over 40 patents for lighting solutions. As the future unfolds, OPEL LED will play a pivotal role in shifting lighting usage in India
